## The Idea

Privacy-first digital notebook membership. $1/28 days. No tracking, no ads, no bullshit. Your data stays yours.

## Problem

Big tech harvests your data. Every note you take, every thought you write - it's being mined, analyzed, sold. People want a simple way to keep private notes without feeding the machine.

## Solution

- $1 membership (28-day cycle)
- Simple, local-first tools
- No accounts required for basic use
- Encrypted by default
- Privacy as the product, not the afterthought
